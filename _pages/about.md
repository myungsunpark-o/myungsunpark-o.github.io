My research focuses on enhancing adaptability and dexterity in robots during physical interaction with complex environments. I draw inspiration from **human motion, sensing, and intelligence**, and embody these principles in real-world robotic systems. My recent work tackles irregular, unsteady, and contact-rich environments through **exteroceptive sensing** (e.g., tactile and flow sensing) and **human–robot interfaces** (e.g., motion-capture gloves and haptic feedback devices) that enable **physical AI for robot manipulation**.
